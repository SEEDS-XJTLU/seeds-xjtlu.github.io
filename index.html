About Us

The Student Economics Exploration & Development Society (S.E.E.D.S.) is a research-oriented student society. We accept both regular members and S.E.E.D.S. Project members for different focuses and tasks.

Provide a Platform

For students to explore economic theories, discuss real-world applications, and develop analytical and research skills.

Bridge the Gap

Connecting classroom learning with practical economic understanding through interactive discussions.

S.E.E.D.S. Project

Driving deep-dive research initiatives and hands-on economic analysis.
