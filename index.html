About Us

The Student Economics Exploration & Development Society (S.E.E.D.S.) is a research-oriented student society dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity, academic excellence, and professional growth in the field of economics and related disciplines. We accept both regular members and S.E.E.D.S. Project members for different focuses and tasks.

Provide a Platform

For students to explore economic theories, discuss real-world applications, and develop analytical and research skills.

Bridge the Gap

Connecting classroom learning with practical economic understanding through interactive discussions.

S.E.E.D.S. Project

Driving deep-dive research initiatives and hands-on economic analysis to empower student researchers.
